Nowadays, most research journals require that data/methods should be transparent and reproducible:

  • Please take a look at the Nature reporting standard nature.com/nature-portfolio/editorial-policies/reporting-standards
  • Examples to check:
    • availability of data (Are the RNA-seq data submitted to public domain like GEO?)
    • code/algorithm availability (Are the codes uploaded in Github or any other code repository?)
    • decent method/material description for other lab to reproduce
